Currency


Currency is the official money of a country that is used to buy goods and services. Each country has its own currency, which depends on its economic condition.





Among the most widely used currencies in the world are the US dollar, the euro, and the British pound. These currencies are often used in international trade and financial operations.



Exchange rates constantly change and are influenced by economic factors, inflation, and international markets. Changes in exchange rates are important for both businesses and ordinary citizens.
