About Us




Our Billiard Club was created out of a deep love and respect for the game of billiards. This is not just a place for entertainment — it is a space where the game becomes a culture and every guest becomes part of our family.




At Billiard Club N, there's something fun for everyone! We have pool tables at an affordable rate, so you can play with your friends all day long. We also offer pool lessons for beginners. Our shop also has video games galore, regulation ping pong tables, and space for events. No matter what you're looking for, we've got the fun covered!
