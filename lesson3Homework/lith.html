Travel to Lithuania


I decided to spend my autumn holidays in Lithuania. I arrived at Vilnius Airport, "Tarptautinis Vilniaus oro uostas". I had planned to visit traditional restaurants and taste their cuisine, as well as explore various streets and landmarks.





On the first day, I was on a beautiful street with lovely decorations, greenery, and lighting. I visited several landmarks.





On the second day, I tasted traditional food, including schnitzel, which was very delicious. I also tried traditional dishes from other countries. The remaining days were rather monotonous; we walked through the streets and enjoyed the autumn days.
