History Of Billiards




The origins of billiards go back to Europe in the 15th century. At first, it was played outdoors on grass, but later it moved indoors and the first tables with pockets were created. Over time, billiards evolved into different styles — Pool, Snooker, Carom, and Russian Pyramid. Today, it is considered a sport of precision, strategy, and intellect.




Our goal is to continue this tradition in a modern, comfortable, and friendly environment. We pay attention to every detail — high-quality equipment, a clean space, and professional service.




With us, billiards is not just a game — it is style, history, and an experience.
