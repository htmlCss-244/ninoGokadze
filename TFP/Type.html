Types of Billiards

Billiards is not just one game â€” it includes different styles and rule sets that vary by table size, number of balls, and objectives. At our club, you can enjoy several popular types of billiards:




Pool

Pool is the most popular and widely played type of billiards. It is played with 15 numbered balls and one white cue ball. The goal is to pocket balls according to the rules. Pool is perfect for both beginners and experienced players.




Snooker

Snooker is considered the most technical and strategic type of billiards. It is played on a large table with 22 balls. It requires precision, patience, and planning. Ideal for those who enjoy a challenge.




Carom (Carambol)

Carom is played on a table without pockets. The goal is to strike other balls with your cue ball following specific rules. This type develops control and accuracy.




Russian Pyramid

Russian billiards uses larger balls and very tight pockets. The game is challenging and requires great skill. It is popular among professional players.




Our club offers excellent conditions for all these types with high-quality equipment and a comfortable environment.Choose your style and play at your best.
