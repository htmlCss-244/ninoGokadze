Biggest Lakes

The largest lake in the world is the Caspian Sea, which is actually a lake, not a sea. It is the biggest by surface area and lies between Europe and Asia. The Caspian Sea is known for its rich nature and large oil resources.

The second and third places belong to Lake Superior and Lake Victoria. Lake Superior is in North America and is the largest freshwater lake in the world by area. Lake Victoria is in Africa and is very important both for its natural resources and for the lives of local people.
